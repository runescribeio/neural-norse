HOW IT WORKS

01
Get a challenge from GET /api/challenge?wallet=YOUR_WALLET
02
Solve the SHA-256 puzzle: find nonce where hash starts with 0000. ~65K iterations, under 1 second for code.
03
POST /api/mint with your wallet, challenge, and nonce. Get back a partially-signed Candy Machine transaction.
04
Sign the transaction with your wallet and submit to Solana. You pay ~0.034 SOL total (mint price + account rent).
